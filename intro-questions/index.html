  8. Adobe Fonts knows who it is and excels at delivering for anyong looking through typefaces. The site takes out a ton of the headache that can come with combing for hours for something specific through Google. It organizes beautifully with panels for type families, promoting creators/foundries, and easily exploring related fonts. The site also functions extremely well by syncing your downloads to your computer for whatever external program you want to input your new typeface.
